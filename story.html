Our Story

The owner and chef, Hotae Sim, began working in the teriyaki business in 1996. The first teriyaki restaurant he owned and ran was Sunny Teriyaki in Green Lake. He then moved to Bothell with his family in 2003. Years later, in 2006, he decided to move his business to Bothell in order to take better care of his two children, Kevin and Casey. Although the business struggled for multiple years, with his wife, Hyun Jung, he persisted and continued to happily serve the Bothell community. Now, both Kevin and Casey are proud Bothell High graduates and the family is honored to serve and be a part of the extraordinary Bothell community.
